At Vibecard, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use **vibecard.co.uk** (the “Site”) and our services.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

When you use Vibecard, we may collect the following information:

- **Account Information**: name, email address, and password when you register.
- **Payment Information**: handled securely by our payment processor (Stripe); we do not store card details.
- **Postcard Information**: recipient name, address, and message content needed to generate and deliver your postcard.
- **Analytics Data**: anonymous site usage data (see Section 6).

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and deliver your postcards.
- Generate AI images as part of our service.
- Process payments securely.
- Communicate with you about your orders or account.
- Improve our website and services.

---

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following lawful bases:

- **Contract**: to provide the services you request.
- **Consent**: where you opt in to marketing communications.
- **Legitimate interests**: to improve our services and ensure site security.

---

## 4. How Long We Keep Your Data

We only keep your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected. Certain information may be retained for legal or tax obligations.

---

## 5. Sharing Your Information

We only share your personal data with trusted partners when needed to provide our service:

- **ClickSend Pty Ltd (Australia)** – used to print and send postcards, which requires sharing your name, address, and message content. ClickSend may transfer data internationally and confirms they only do so with appropriate safeguards and contractual protections in place.
- **Stripe Payments UK, Ltd.** – handles payment processing securely. Stripe is a data controller in its own right.
- **Simple Analytics** – provides anonymised site usage data without tracking cookies or personal identifiers.

---

## 6. Analytics

We use **Simple Analytics**, a privacy-friendly analytics provider. Simple Analytics does not use cookies or collect personal data. Instead, it gives us aggregated, anonymised insights into how people use our site. You can learn more at [simpleanalytics.com](https://simpleanalytics.com).

---

## 7. Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you.
- Request correction of inaccurate information.
- Request deletion of your personal data.
- Object to or restrict how we process your data.
- Request a copy of your data in a portable format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at **support@vibecard.co.uk**.

---

## 8. Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to safeguard your data against loss, theft, or misuse. However, no online service can be completely secure.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
